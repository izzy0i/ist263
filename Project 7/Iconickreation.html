Understanding Hair Types

Description: “Hair comes in a variety of textures and curl patterns, with natural Black hair showcasing beautiful diversity. From soft, defined curls (like 4A) to tight, densely packed coils (like 4C), each type requires unique care. Hydration, protective styles, and gentle detangling are key to maintaining growth and health. Some styles, like intricate braids or twist-outs, look especially stunning on coily and curly textures, highlighting the beauty and versatility of Black hair.”

The shape of the hair follicle determines the texture of the hair. • Asian Hair: Straight and sleek, with round follicles that create a smooth flow. • Caucasian Hair: Varies from straight to wavy or curly, shaped by slightly oval follicles. • Afro-Textured Hair: Tight curls and coils come from elliptical follicles, offering volume and versatility. Each texture thrives with tailored care, and certain styles—like sleek ponytails, beach waves, or defined curls—work best depending on the hair type, making it important to embrace your natural beauty and choose styles that enhance your unique texture.”
